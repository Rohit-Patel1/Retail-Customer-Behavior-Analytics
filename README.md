## Overview

This project is an interactive Business Intelligence dashboard developed using **Power BI, SQL, and DAX** to analyze customer purchasing behavior and generate actionable business insights. The dashboard helps businesses understand customer demographics, revenue trends, subscription performance, product preferences, seasonal sales patterns, and customer loyalty.

The analysis is based on a retail customer dataset containing 3,900+ customer records and multiple customer-related attributes.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze customer purchasing behavior
* Identify high-value customer segments
* Track revenue and sales performance
* Evaluate subscription effectiveness
* Discover seasonal purchasing trends
* Compare product category performance
* Analyze geographic revenue distribution
* Support data-driven business decisions
